FAQs

Questions? We've Got Answers

Everything you need to know about working with The Social Circle.

How fast will I receive my content?

Our turnaround time depends on your package. Typically, you'll receive your first batch of content within 7-10 business days after our initial strategy session. Once we're in full swing, you'll get regular weekly or bi-weekly deliveries as outlined in your plan. Rush requests can be accommodated—just let us know!

How do I request specific content or revisions?

All our clients get access to a dedicated WhatsApp group (Growth & Premium packages) or direct communication channel. You can share ideas, reference videos, or request changes anytime. We offer unlimited revisions on most packages to ensure you're 100% satisfied with the final product.

Why wouldn't I just hire my own in-house team?

Hiring an in-house team means salaries, benefits, equipment, training, and overhead—easily £60k+ per year for just one specialist. With The Social Circle, you get an entire team of experts (videographers, editors, strategists, designers) for a fraction of the cost. Plus, we bring 4+ years of experience, proven strategies, and a network of influencers that would take years to build internally.

What if I'm not happy with the results?

Your satisfaction is our priority. We offer unlimited revisions on content to ensure it meets your vision. If you're not seeing the growth or engagement we promised, we'll adjust our strategy at no extra cost. Most packages are month-to-month, so there's no long-term lock-in—though we've never had a client leave after seeing our results!

Do you offer trials or sample work before committing?

We don't typically offer free trials, but we do provide case studies, portfolio examples, and client testimonials so you can see our proven track record. For serious inquiries, we can discuss a discounted first month or a pilot project to demonstrate our capabilities. Book a call with us to explore your options!

What's included in social media management?

Full social media management includes content creation (reels, posts, stories), caption writing, hashtag research, posting schedules, community engagement (responding to comments/DMs), performance analytics, and monthly strategy reviews. On higher-tier packages, we also handle ad campaigns, influencer outreach, and creative direction.

Can I cancel or pause my subscription?

Yes! Most of our packages are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel or pause your subscription anytime with 30 days' notice. We're confident in our work, so we don't believe in locking clients into lengthy agreements. Your success keeps you with us, not a contract.

Which platforms do you specialize in?

We specialize in Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook—the platforms where visual storytelling thrives. We also support YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn (for B2B clients), and emerging platforms. Our expertise lies in short-form video content, which is dominating all social media algorithms right now. If you need coverage on a specific platform, let's discuss it!
